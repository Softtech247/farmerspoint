About Us

Welcome to Farmers Point, your number one virtual marketplace that seamlessly connects you to farmers that have a wide range of free produces readily available at your fingertips. Our goal at Farmers Point is to give our valuable customers an easy, sustainable option to buy fresh, local goods and assist small-scale farmers.
Moreover, we pride ourselves in our commitment to providing a huge selection of produce that are fresh, organic and also locally sourced. We work hard to provide a wide variety of goods that live up to our strict standards for quality and sustainability, ranging from seasonal fruits and vegetables to grass-fed meats and handmade goods.

Our Story

Farmers Point is an online platform that connects farmers and consumers, bridging the gap between rural and urban communities. It is more than just an online farmers market. Our journey began with a straightforward goal: to support smallholder farmers all over Nigeria while making it simpler for Nigerians to get fresh and healthful goods.
No matter where they live, everyone should have access to wholesome food, and this is the bedrock of Farmers Point. We are thrilled to be a part of Nigeria's agriculture sector reform and look forward to strengthening farmer empowerment and transforming the nation's food access.

Goal and Mission

Our goal is to give Nigerian farmers a reliable and sustainable platform to sell their fresh produce to customers directly while supporting rural development and sustainable agriculture. We want all Nigerians to have access to food that is produced locally, is healthy, and is reasonably priced.
Our mission is to be the top online farmers market in Nigeria, empowering smallholder farmers and revolutionizing the agricultural industry, fostering a culture of healthy eating and food security while assisting local farmers and communities. In order to enable a more resilient and sustainable food system for our future generations, we work to create a platform that encourages trust, collaboration, and innovation.
